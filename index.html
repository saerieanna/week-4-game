Crystals Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game. There are four

crystals below. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your current

number. Win a round by matching your current number to the target number. You will lose

the round if your total score goes above the target number. To win the game, you must

reach a score of 50 points before your 5 chances are up. For each round you win, you

earn 10 points, and for each round you lose, you will lose 10 points, AND your chances

left to play will decrease. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you

click on it. Oh, and each time the game starts, the value of each crystal changes.

Good Luck!
